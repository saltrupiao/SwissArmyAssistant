The Swiss Army Assistant: Mankind's Next Step

Fire, the steam engine, the personal computer, the internet, the smart phone. Now it's time for mankind to take its next step

Introducing: The Swiss Army Assistant. All the utility of your Swiss Army knife, in the palm of your hand, but now on a screen.

Key Features:

  • A Calculator, able to perform over two billion different mathmatical equations
  • Real time weather tracking, with helpful graphics for the numerically impaired
  • Unlimited* cloud storage
  • Notepad, with support for every letter in the English language, and beyond
  • Dynamic and versatile themes featuring over two colors
  • A music player which could play grammy award winning musical tracks
  • Integrated news from reputable journalists and Fox

*Unlimited cloud storage must not exceed 4Gb
